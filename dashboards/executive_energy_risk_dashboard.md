# Executive energy risk dashboard

## Purpose

The executive energy risk dashboard is designed to give a simplified view of regional energy operations risk.

## Main view

Source view:

- `vattenfall_dev.analytics.vw_executive_energy_risk_dashboard`

## Suggested sections

### 1. Regional risk overview

Shows executive risk status by day and region.

### 2. Market pressure days

Highlights days with elevated market price pressure.

### 3. Weather-grid risk context

Shows where weather risk and grid incidents appear together.

### 4. Asset incident intelligence

Highlights assets or regions with repeated or elevated incidents.

### 5. Executive risk status

Provides a simplified status such as:

- `NORMAL`
- `WATCH`
- `ATTENTION`
- `CRITICAL`

## Interpretation note

The dashboard is designed for executive interpretation. It simplifies operational signals without exposing unnecessary technical details.
