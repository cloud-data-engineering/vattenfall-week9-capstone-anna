# Day 4 Night Shift scope

The Day 4 Night Shift is an advanced extension of the normal Day 4 delivery layer.

It does not replace the existing Day 4 Gold outputs. Instead, it adds a second advanced delivery layer focused on trust, risk intelligence, observability, governance, and executive reporting.

## Purpose

The Night Shift extension should make the capstone more:

- trustworthy
- analytical
- explainable
- governed
- presentation-ready

## Starting point

The Night Shift builds on the existing project outputs:

- cleaned Silver tables in `vattenfall_dev.refined`
- normal Day 4 Gold tables in `vattenfall_dev.analytics`
- existing dashboard-ready Gold output
- existing governance structure in Unity Catalog

## Advanced focus areas

The extension adds:

- structured data trust auditing
- asset-level incident intelligence
- weather-grid risk correlation
- market-operations stress analysis
- executive energy risk dashboard view
- pipeline observability summary
- governance documentation extension

## Important principle

This is not a repetition of the normal Day 4 work. The goal is to add deeper trust and risk intelligence on top of the existing delivery layer.
