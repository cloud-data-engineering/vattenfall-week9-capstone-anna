# Business context

This project simulates a Vattenfall-inspired energy operations and market intelligence lakehouse.

The business goal is to combine multiple energy-related data sources into a governed Databricks lakehouse that supports operational and analytical use cases.

## Source domains

The project uses four source domains:

- market prices
- weather observations
- grid events
- reference data

## Business value

Market price data can help identify energy price patterns and market volatility.

Weather data can help explain operational pressure caused by temperature, wind, precipitation, or alerts.

Grid event data can help track incidents, asset issues, severity, and duration.

Reference data helps enrich operational records with asset and region context.

## Final project direction

The final lakehouse should support reporting outputs that explain energy operations, market intelligence, and operational risk indicators.
