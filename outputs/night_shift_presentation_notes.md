# Night Shift presentation notes

## Why this extension exists

The normal Day 4 layer creates business-facing Gold outputs. The Night Shift extension adds a more advanced assurance and risk intelligence layer.

## What the Night Shift adds

The Night Shift adds:

- trust audit evidence
- asset-level incident intelligence
- weather-grid risk signals
- market-operations stress signals
- executive-facing risk view
- structured observability summary

## How this differs from normal Day 4

Normal Day 4 answers:

- what happened by day and region?
- what Gold outputs can feed dashboards?

Night Shift answers:

- which outputs can be trusted?
- which assets or regions need attention?
- which risks combine across market, weather, and operations?
- what should executives see quickly?
- what evidence shows the pipeline ran correctly?

## Important caveat

The weather-grid and market-operations outputs show aligned operational signals. They do not prove causality.
